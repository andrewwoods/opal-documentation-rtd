
Bash Clocks
===========

What time is it? There are many sources to find the answer. Opal makes it a bit
easier to display the current time in the different parts of the world on the
command line. The `~/opal/bash/aliases-clocks.bash` contains many aliases
defined for you. Find the ones that best suit your needs. While there are many
provided, the one you need may not be available. You can use these examples to
define your own. Add your custom definition into your dotfiles, rather than
updating Opal.

In the install script, a clocks alias was added to your `$HOME/.bashrc` file.
That was provided as a starting point. You should update it to meet your needs.
